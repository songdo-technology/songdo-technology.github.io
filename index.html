Google Calendar

  1. Click "+" next to "Other calendars"
  2. Select "From URL"
  3. Paste the subscription URL
  4. Click "Add calendar"

iPhone/iPad

  1. Settings → Calendar → Accounts
  2. Add Account → Other
  3. Add Calendar Subscription
  4. Enter the subscription URL

Mac Calendar

  1. File → New Calendar Subscription
  2. Enter the subscription URL
  3. Click "Subscribe"
  4. Choose your preferences

Outlook

  1. Add calendar → Subscribe from web
  2. Paste the subscription URL
  3. Name your calendar
  4. Click "Import"

Calendar FAQ

Update Delays

  • Google Calendar: Every 8-24 hours
  • iPhone/iPad: Every 15 minutes to several hours
  • Outlook: Every 3 hours by default
  • Apple Calendar: Every 15 minutes to 1 hour

Update delays depend on each server's load management configuration, not our system. Timing varies based on each server status

Force Refresh

  • Google Calendar: Press Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) in browser
  • iPhone/iPad: Open Calendar app → Pull down to refresh
  • Mac Calendar: View menu → Refresh Calendars or press Cmd+R
  • Outlook: Right-click calendar name → "Update Folder" or press F9

Still Seeing Old Events?

  1. Wait 15-30 minutes for cache to clear
  2. Clear your browser cache
  3. Remove subscription, wait 10 minutes, re-add
  4. Check if you're viewing the correct date range
